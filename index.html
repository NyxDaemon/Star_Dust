About Me

I am a Master's graduate in Astrophysics and Cosmology from the Alma Mater Studiorum - Università di Bologna, currently conducting research on dust formation in core-collapse supernovae. My work bridges stellar evolution, supernova progenitors, and data-driven astrophysics, with experience across spectroscopy, high-energy astrophysics, and HPC. I am passionate about applying ML and cloud computing to big data problems in astrophysics.
